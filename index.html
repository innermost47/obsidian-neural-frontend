Draw Your Sound

Transform visual drawings into unique audio samples with AI

Sketch-to-Audio Generation

Draw-to-Audio Live Demo

This YouTube video requires cookies to be displayed.

Raw improvisation showcasing the experimental draw-to-audio workflow: sketch patterns → AI interprets visuals → generates matching loops on the fly

1. Sketch Your Idea

Use drawing tools (Pencil, Brush, Spray, Eraser) and 10 colors on a 512x512 canvas. Visual patterns translate to sonic characteristics.

  • Pencil → Sharp, staccato sounds
  • Brush → Smooth, sustained pads
  • Colors → Frequency ranges & timbres

2. Add Musical Keywords

Select from 20+ musical keywords (drums, techno, ambient, synth...) or add your own. Keywords guide the AI interpretation.

  • Pre-defined musical terms
  • Custom keywords support
  • Alphabetically sorted & scrollable

3. AI Sonic Translation

Vision LLM analyzes your drawing: tools, colors, patterns, composition. Generates detailed musical prompt for audio synthesis.

  • Visual → Sonic interpretation
  • Context-aware generation
  • BPM & Key synchronized

Visual-to-Sonic Mapping

Colors → Frequencies
  • ● Black → Deep bass, sub-bass
  • ● Red → Aggressive mid-high energy
  • ● Blue → Cool, ethereal, reverberant
  • ● Yellow → Bright, high frequencies
Patterns → Rhythms
  • Vertical lines → Staccato, percussive hits
  • Horizontal flows → Sustained notes, drones
  • Circular patterns → Arpeggios, sequences
  • Chaotic scribbles → Glitchy, randomized
Drawing State Saved: Your canvas, selected keywords, and brush settings are saved per track and per page (A/B/C/D) in your project.
